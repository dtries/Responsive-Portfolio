About Me

Dennis is a student in the University of Denver Coding Bootcamp for Full-Stack Web Development. His background is in psychological acoustics and hearing science where he gained a passion for programming while writing code for experiments. During this time Dennis gained a particular interest in intuitive graphical user interfaces. He considers himself a life-long learner and is enthusiatic about discovering ways to combine his interests in sensory psychology and memory with the ongoing evolution of web-development.

During his time as an univeristy faculty member, Dennis authored 18 scientific articles in well-regarded, peer-reviewed journals. He has also presented his research findings at national and international scientific conventions. Additionally, Dennis has worked clinically as a diagnostic audiologist for some of the top otolaryngolists in the country.

Dennis is currently following his passion for programming and web development. He is always intersted in taking on challanging and innovative projects. Reach out to Dennis via the contact section of this website to connect!
